Commonly Asked Questions

Straight answers to the things homeowners ask us most.

Will a cash offer be lower than listing?

Often, yes. The tradeoff is speed, certainty, privacy, and avoiding repairs, showings, commissions, and extra months of holding costs.

Do I have to clean or fix anything?

No. We evaluate the property in its current condition and factor repairs into the offer instead of asking you to complete them first.

How fast can closing happen?

That depends on title, local requirements, and your situation. If everything is clear, as soon as 14 days.

Am I committed after requesting options?

No. The first conversation is meant to help you understand your choices. You decide whether the offer makes sense.

What if I have a mortgage or liens?

We work with title to pay off existing mortgages, liens, or judgments at closing from the proceeds. You walk with the net.

Can I stay in the house after closing?

Yes. Rent-back agreements are common—we can build a lease-back into the terms so you have time to move.

Do you buy houses in any condition?

Yes. Fire damage, foundation issues, hoarding, code violations, vacant, tenant-occupied—we've seen it all and factor it into the offer.

Is there any fee to get an offer?

No. Requesting an offer is free, no-obligation, and there are no hidden fees.
