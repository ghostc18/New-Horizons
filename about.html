We started New Horizons after watching too many homeowners get stuck between a rock and a hard place—needing to sell fast but not wanting to give away equity to someone who treats the transaction like a volume game.

Our Commitment in Writing

Every offer we make includes a one-page summary: offer price, estimated repairs, closing costs, and your projected net proceeds. No fine print. No surprises at the closing table.
