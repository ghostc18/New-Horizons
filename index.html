The New Horizons difference

Questions

The things sellers actually ask.

Will a cash offer be lower than listing?

Often, yes. The tradeoff is speed, certainty, privacy, and avoiding repairs, showings, commissions, and extra months of holding costs.

Do I have to clean or fix anything?

No. We evaluate the property in its current condition and factor repairs into the offer instead of asking you to complete them first.

How fast can closing happen?

That depends on title, local requirements, and your situation. If everything is clear, cash sales can often move much faster than financed deals.

Am I committed after requesting options?

No. The first conversation is meant to help you understand your choices. You decide whether the offer makes sense.
