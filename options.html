Do-It-Yourself

For Sale By Owner (FSBO)

Can save on listing commission and gives you control, but you handle pricing, marketing, showings, negotiation, contracts, and buyer financing risk yourself.

  • No listing commission (2.5–3%)
  • You control showings & pricing
  • You handle all marketing
  • You negotiate directly
  • Buyer agent commission still typical
  • Legal/compliance on you
